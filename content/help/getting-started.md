# Getting Started with Neon SEO Beacon

Welcome to Neon SEO Beacon! This guide will help you get up and running with our professional SEO audit tool in just a few minutes.

## Creating Your Account

### Step 1: Sign Up

1. Visit [neonseobeacon.com](https://neonseobeacon.com)
2. Click "Start Free Trial" in the top right corner
3. Enter your email address and create a secure password
4. Verify your email address by clicking the link we send you

### Step 2: Complete Your Profile

1. Add your company name and website
2. Select your primary use case (Agency, Business, Personal)
3. Choose your plan (you can always upgrade later)

## Running Your First Audit

### Quick Audit

1. From the dashboard, click "New Audit"
2. Enter your website URL (e.g., https://example.com)
3. Select your audit preferences:
   - **Desktop/Mobile**: Choose which version to analyze
   - **Region**: Select your target geographic region
   - **Depth**: Choose between quick or comprehensive analysis
4. Click "Start Audit"

### Understanding the Progress

Watch your audit progress in real-time:

- **Crawling**: We're fetching your website
- **Analyzing**: Running 50+ SEO checks
- **AI Processing**: Claude AI is generating recommendations
- **Finalizing**: Preparing your report

Most audits complete within 30-60 seconds.

## Understanding Your Results

### SEO Score

Your overall SEO score (0-100) is calculated based on:

- **Technical SEO** (40%): Meta tags, site structure, crawlability
- **Performance** (30%): Core Web Vitals, page speed
- **Content** (20%): Content quality, keyword optimization
- **Mobile** (10%): Mobile-friendliness, responsive design

## Best Practices

### Before Running Audits

1. **Ensure your website is live** and accessible
2. **Check that important pages aren't blocked** by robots.txt
3. **Consider auditing key landing pages** individually
4. **Plan regular audits** to track progress

### Interpreting Results

1. **Start with high-impact issues** marked as "Critical"
2. **Focus on technical problems first** before content optimization
3. **Use AI recommendations** to understand the reasoning
4. **Track progress over time** with regular audits

### Taking Action

1. **Prioritize by impact** - fix issues that will improve your score most
2. **Address technical issues first** - these often have the biggest impact
3. **Implement changes gradually** - test changes before full deployment
4. **Monitor results** - run follow-up audits to measure improvement

## Common Questions

### How often should I run audits?

- **After major website changes**: Always audit after significant updates
- **Monthly**: For active websites with regular content updates
- **Quarterly**: For stable websites with minimal changes
- **Weekly**: For websites under active SEO optimization

### What if my score is low?

Don't worry! A low score simply means there are opportunities for improvement:

1. Focus on the "Critical" issues first
2. Follow our AI recommendations step by step
3. Implement changes gradually
4. Run follow-up audits to track progress

### Can I audit any website?

Yes! You can audit:

- Your own websites
- Client websites (with permission)
- Competitor websites (for analysis)
- Any publicly accessible website
